Ukulele Toys

I play a surprising amount of Ukulele these days. My favourite folks to strum with are the fantastic crew of Ukulele Wednesdays, and the amazing Learn to Uke alumni band.

I make them things for fun. I get to learn bits of music theory (and whatever tech I'm moved to play with), and they get a new shiny toy.

  • Ukulele chord-o-matic

    A tool to format songs in the Ukulele Wednesdays house style, including chord diagrams, with a minimum of fuss.

    Chord-o-matic | Code

  • Song Randomizer

    Can't work out what song we need next? This handy tool will help your requestor's block.

    Randomizer | Code

  • Love Machine Autotweeter

    For Valentine's Day 2018, we wanted a bot to automatically request all the love songs it knew. I had busted my calf skiing, and so @UkeLoveMachine was born of my downtime.

    Twitter Account | Code
